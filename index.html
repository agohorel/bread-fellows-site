Where have all the bakeries gone?

Every town, big and small, once had a bakery - before super markets took on the task of making and selling bread.


Bread Fellows grew out of our love of bread, a desire to learn the craft of bread making, and a need for locally made bread. We are making bread with organic flours and grains.
